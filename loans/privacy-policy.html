Privacy Policy for LoanTracker

Last Updated: January 12, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to LoanTracker ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application LoanTracker.

LoanTracker is a free, personal finance tool designed for individual use to track loans and manage financial records.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

When you use LoanTracker, we collect:

  • Email address - Used for account creation and authentication via Google OAuth
  • Display name - Your name from your Google account or as you provide it
  • Profile picture - Optional, from your Google account

2.2 Financial Data

You create and store the following information in LoanTracker:

  • Loan details (amounts, dates, interest rates, counterparty names)
  • Transaction records (payments, receipts)
  • Notes and tags associated with loans
  • Currency and date preferences

2.3 Technical Information

  • Device timezone
  • App version and platform information
  • Authentication tokens (stored securely)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Provide app functionality - Enable you to track and manage your loans
  • Authenticate you - Verify your identity when you log in
  • Store your data - Save your loan records securely
  • Sync across devices - Access your data from multiple devices

We do NOT use your data for advertising, marketing, or any commercial purposes.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (a secure cloud database service):

  • Encryption in transit - All data is transmitted over HTTPS
  • Encryption at rest - Your data is encrypted in the database
  • Access control - Only you can access your loan data
  • Row-level security - Database policies ensure users can only see their own data

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • Supabase (Database Provider) - To store and manage your data securely
  • Google OAuth - For authentication purposes only
  • Legal requirements - If required by law or to protect our rights

We do NOT share your financial data with anyone for marketing, analytics, or commercial purposes.

6. Your Data Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data - Export all your loan data via CSV
  • Modify your data - Edit or delete loans and transactions at any time
  • Delete your account - Request complete account deletion (contact us)
  • Data portability - Export your data in CSV format

7. Data Retention

  • Your loan data is retained as long as your account is active
  • You can delete individual loans or transactions at any time
  • If you delete your account, all associated data will be permanently removed

8. Children's Privacy

LoanTracker is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18.

9. Google OAuth and Contacts

We use Google OAuth for secure authentication. When you log in with Google:

  • We request access to your email and basic profile
  • Optional: Access to Google Contacts (only if you choose to use contact suggestion features)
  • Contact data is only used within the app to help you add counterparty names
  • We do NOT store or upload your contacts to our servers

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

11. Third-Party Services

LoanTracker uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase - Database and authentication (Privacy Policy)
  • Google OAuth - Authentication (Privacy Policy)

12. Disclaimer

LoanTracker is provided "as is" for personal use only.

  • This app is a personal finance tool and NOT legal or financial advice
  • We are not responsible for any financial decisions made using this app
  • Loan records created in this app are for personal tracking only and may not be legally binding
  • We recommend consulting with legal or financial professionals for formal loan agreements
