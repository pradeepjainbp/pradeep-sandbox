What I do

I run subscriber marketing operations for some of America's most recognised magazine brands — including Better Homes & Gardens, Midwest Living and Flea Market Décor — from Bangalore, India. Seven to ten campaigns a year, millions of pieces mailed, and every number tracked.

What I actually love is the system underneath the campaign — the forecast model, the automated dashboard, the macro that saves three hours every Monday. I've spent 18 years building those, first at Thomson Reuters, then across the Time Inc → Meredith → Dotdash Meredith journey.

Lately I've been teaching myself Python and building AI agent systems to automate the work I used to do manually. You can see some of that in the other tabs on this site.
